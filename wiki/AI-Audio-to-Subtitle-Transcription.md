# AI Audio to Subtitle Transcription

Convert speech in audio and video files into highly accurate text subtitles using AI-powered audio-to-subtitle transcription. The AI Opensubtitles Desktop Client makes it incredibly easy to auto-generate SRT and VTT files from nearly any media format.

## Supported Input Formats for Transcription

### Video to Text
Easily extract audio and generate subtitles from MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WMV, WebM, FLV, 3GP, and over 20 additional video formats.

### Audio to Text
Transcribe spoken words to subtitles from MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG, M4A, WMA, AIFF, and over 15 other audio formats.

## How to Transcribe a File

### Step 1: Select Media File
- **Drag & drop** an audio or video file directly onto the application window.
- Or click to open your file browser and select the media file you want to transcribe.

### Step 2: Configure Transcription Options
- **Language**: Choose the spoken language, or enable automatic language detection if you aren't sure.
- **AI Model**: Select from multiple state-of-the-art AI transcription models (choose based on your desired quality vs. speed).
- **Output Format**: Choose to export your subtitles as **SRT** or **VTT**.
- **Return Content**: Enable or disable this option to receive the full, raw transcription text.

### Step 3: Process the Audio
Click **"Start Transcription"** to begin the automated AI generation process.

### Step 4: Monitor Progress
Watch the real-time progress bar in the status area. The app actively displays:
- Current operation status (e.g., uploading, transcribing)
- Credit balance updates
- Overall processing progress

### Step 5: Review & Save Subtitles
- Preview the auto-generated subtitles within the app, complete with syntax highlighting.
- Export the final SRT or VTT file to your desired folder location.

## Automatic Language Detection

The AI auto subtitle generator can automatically detect the primary spoken language in your media file:
- Enable auto-detection when dealing with unknown source material.
- Saves significant time when processing large batches of multi-language content.

## Tips for the Best Auto Subtitle Generation

1. **Audio Quality**: High-quality, clear audio produces the most accurate transcriptions with fewer errors.
2. **Multiple Speakers**: The advanced AI seamlessly handles multiple speakers automatically.
3. **Background Noise**: Minimal background music or noise dramatically improves transcription accuracy.
4. **Accents**: Select the appropriate language variant (e.g., US English vs. UK English) if available for better results.
