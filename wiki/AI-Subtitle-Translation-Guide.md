# AI Subtitle Translation Guide

Translate existing subtitle files between over 100 languages quickly and accurately. Learn how to translate SRT files and VTT files using advanced AI models built into the AI Opensubtitles Desktop Client.

## Supported Subtitle Input Formats

- **SRT (SubRip Text)**: The industry standard subtitle format.
- **VTT (WebVTT)**: The preferred format for modern web video players.

Both file formats fully support Unicode, ensuring that international characters, symbols, and diverse alphabets are handled correctly during translation.

## How to Translate Subtitles

### Step 1: Select Your Subtitle File
Choose an existing subtitle file (SRT or VTT) that you wish to translate:
- **Drag & drop** the file onto the application window.
- Or click to browse your computer and select the file.

### Step 2: Configure Translation Languages
- **Source Language**: Set the original language of the text, or enable auto-detection.
- **Target Language**: Choose from 100+ available target languages for your new subtitles.
- **Translation Model**: Select your preferred AI translation engine (e.g., DeepL).

### Step 3: Process Translation
Click **"Start Translation"** to begin processing your SRT or VTT file.

### Step 4: Monitor Status
Track the translation progress with live updates and monitor your remaining API credits in the status bar.

### Step 5: Review & Export
- Preview the newly translated subtitle content directly within the application.
- Export and save the translated subtitles to your desired location on your computer.

## Auto-Detection for Subtitles

Enable automatic language detection when:
- You are unsure of the original source language of an SRT or VTT file.
- You are working with a large directory of multilingual content.
- The source language occasionally varies within the same file.

## Tips for Best Translation Results

1. **Original Quality**: Better, accurately timed original subtitles produce much better AI translations.
2. **Formatting Preservation**: VTT and SRT specific formatting (like italics or bolding) is safely preserved during the AI translation process.
3. **Special Characters**: Complete Unicode support ensures international characters and complex scripts are handled flawlessly.
4. **Context Matters**: Advanced AI translation models consider sentence context to provide much higher accuracy than traditional word-for-word translators.
